About us.

We are DAM students making our proyect a reality

At GolVision, we believe football is more than just a sport — it's emotion, strategy, and community. It's a universal language that connects millions of people around the world. That’s why we set out to build a platform that lives up to that passion. From the beginning, our goal was clear: to create a space where fans could follow live scores in real time and anticipate match outcomes using prediction tools powered by real data.

The idea behind GolVision was born from a shared need among fans, bettors, and sports analysts: a reliable, intuitive, and dynamic platform that not only informs but also helps make sense of the game from a deeper, data-driven perspective. We realized that many apps provided scores, but few offered real insight or foresight. That’s when GolVision came to life, a fusion of technology, statistics, and a true love for football.
